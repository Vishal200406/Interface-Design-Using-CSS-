Interesting Facts

  • Dairy Milk was first introduced in 1905 by Cadbury.
  • It has a higher proportion of milk than other chocolate bars.
  • One of the best-selling chocolate brands in the UK.
  • Available in various flavors like Caramel, Fruit & Nut, and more.
  • The iconic purple wrapper is synonymous with the brand.
